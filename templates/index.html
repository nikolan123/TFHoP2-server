A preservation server restoring retired online features used by Geoff Keighley's interactive ebook, The Final Hours of Portal 2.

Download the patcher How to install

Restored services

  • PollsOriginal questions with fixed backend.
  • FeedbackThe final comments page, including preserved historical posts.
  • AudioLegacy preview addresses redirected to surviving music previews.
  • VideoA compatibility player for the ebook's obsolete YouTube Flash API.
  • InteractivesRecovered diagrams and web-based interactive features.
